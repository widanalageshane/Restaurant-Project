About Us

Welcome to Currybawl! We're a group of students who love tasty food and decided to share our passion with Oulu. We opened our doors on May 2, 2024, and since then, we've been serving up tasty dishes inspired by Sri Lankan, Indian, and Chinese flavors.

At Currybawl, we believe in keeping things simple and delicious. Our menu is filled with dishes that pack a punch of flavor, from spicy curries to hearty gravies and savory stir-fries. We're all about creating a cozy, friendly atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

If you're looking for a place to enjoy some amazing food and good company, come on over to Currybawl. We can't wait to welcome you and treat you to a dining experience you won't forget!
